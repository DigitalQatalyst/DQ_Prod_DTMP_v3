The Digital Transformation Metamodel Platform (DTMP) is an internal enterprise platform designed to accelerate an organization's digital transformation journey toward becoming a Digital-Centric Organization (DCO).

2. THIS MARKETPLACE: LEARNING CENTER
What is Learning Center?
Learning Center is the organizational learning and development hub for digital transformation. It provides structured courses, certifications, and learning paths designed to upskill the workforce on transformation methodologies, technologies, and best practices.
Core Function: Deliver comprehensive, sequential education programs with progress tracking, assessments, and certification to build transformation capabilities across the organization.
Why Does This Marketplace Exist?

â€¢	âŒ Skills gaps - Workforce lacks transformation skills (cloud, APIs, agile, etc.)
â€¢	âŒ Inconsistent training - Teams learn from random sources (YouTube, blogs, vendor docs)
â€¢	âŒ No standardization - Everyone learns different approaches, creates confusion
â€¢	âŒ Poor tracking - No visibility into who has what skills or training
â€¢	âŒ Onboarding challenges - New hires struggle to learn organizational standards
â€¢	âŒ Expensive external training - Paying for external courses repeatedly
â€¢	âŒ Knowledge silos - Experts exist but don't share knowledge systematically

â€¢	âœ… Curated curriculum - TO-created courses aligned to transformation strategy
â€¢	âœ… Structured learning - Progressive courses with lessons, quizzes, projects
â€¢	âœ… Certification programs - Recognized credentials for key competencies
â€¢	âœ… Learning paths - Guided journeys for roles (architect, developer, PM)
â€¢	âœ… Progress tracking - Visibility into individual and team learning
â€¢	âœ… Internal experts - Leverage organizational knowledge through courses
â€¢	âœ… Cost efficiency - Build once, train many, reduce external training costs
â€¢	âœ… Consistent standards - Everyone learns the same organizational approaches

### **Key RBAC Principles**

**Open Course Access:**
- All internal users can view and enroll in all courses
- Promotes learning culture
- No artificial barriers to skill development

**Manager Oversight:**
- Managers can see team members' progress
- Can assign training to team
- Cannot view progress outside their team

**TO Content Control:**
- Only TO can create/edit courses
- Quality control for curriculum
- Consistent standards

**Certificate Authority:**
- Certificates issued automatically by system
- TO can revoke if needed (fraud, policy violation)
- Public verification for authenticity

**External Partners:**
- Limited course catalog (partner onboarding, tools training)
- No access to internal strategic content
- Cannot see other learners' progress
